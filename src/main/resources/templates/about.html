About Slice House

Crafting authentic Italian pizzas since 1995

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Slice House began as a small family-owned pizzeria with a simple mission: to bring authentic Italian pizza to our community. What started as a dream in a modest kitchen has grown into a beloved local institution.

Our founder, Giuseppe Romano, brought his family's century-old recipes from Naples, Italy. Using traditional wood-fired ovens and the finest ingredients, we've maintained that authentic taste while serving thousands of happy customers.

Today, Slice House continues to honor those traditions while embracing innovation. Every pizza is handcrafted with care, using locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible, and baked to perfection in our signature wood-fired ovens.
